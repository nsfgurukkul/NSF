About NSF!

Who We Are:

We're India's first startup fellowship for young minds.
We help you learn how to build a startup, lead a team, and think
like an entrepreneur.

What We Do:

  • Teach startup thinking
  • Connect you with real mentors
  • Help you turn ideas into action
  • Make learning fun, real, and exciting

Our Mission:

To turn curious students into confident changemakers
